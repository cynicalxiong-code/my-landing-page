ABOUT  US

ClimeCore Co., Ltd.

Headquartered in the Eastern Science and Technology Innovation Park in Hangzhou, Zhejiang, Sunchine Environmental is a specialized HVAC design, manufacturing and R&D company focused on air dehumidification, integrating solution design, production and project support.

Our main products include pool dehumidification heat pumps, rotary dehumidifiers, constant temperature and humidity units, constant humidity units, temperature-control dehumidifiers and fresh-air dehumidification air conditioners. We provide complete air temperature and humidity solutions for every customer.


Since its establishment, Sunchine has followed a reliable working principle: every request is answered, every task is followed through and every matter receives feedback. This standard gives customers confidence when they entrust projects to us.
